About Accel

Accel Limited was started in 1991 as a multi-vendor IT Services Company in Chennai. It evolved over the past three decades, creating niche entities on a versatile range of domains to meet the requirements of the discerning IT user community with state of the art solutions and services.

Accel offers a wide range of services that include IT Infrastructure Management services, Warranty Management Services, Managed Print Services, Cyber Security, and Software Services, with strong presence in the domestic and international markets. Our businesses also include Accel Media, Accel Realty, Accel Academy and Cetronics Technologies.

The visionary behind Accel limited is Mr. N.R. Panicker, who is the Founder, Chairman and Managing Director. Mr. Panicker is a successful serial entrepreneur and mentor to many aspiring Entrepreneurs. His passion for innovation and unwavering focus on providing excellent customer service has led this company to be one of the most successful technology services company in the industry. We strive to create meaningful differences and an enduring value to our customers.
